My journey to Design

I got into UCSD as a biology major student and,for the previous two years, I had been switching between majors under the biology department, struggling to settle on any of them. After much searching online and asking advisors and friends, I decided to take an online UIUX course to try it out. The opportunity of digging into the real problems and exploring various solutions through itering user experience research has been an inspiring and fascinating experience for me. Moreover, with over 10 years of learning sketching and 2 years of biology background, I also developed a huge interest in UI design. I enjoyed the appealing visual elements as well as the underlying physiological and psychological effects of how we perceive the world. All those factors have prepared me as a more thoughtful, well rounded and creative designer. And that is how I started my journey into Design.
